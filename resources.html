Free YouTube Courses & Playlists

World-class ML education from top universities and industry experts - completely free

Neural Networks: Zero to Hero

Andrej Karpathy

10/10

Former Tesla AI Director and OpenAI founding member teaches neural networks from absolute scratch. Build GPT, backprop, and transformers by hand. The gold standard for understanding deep learning internals.

Why you need this: Interviewers love candidates who understand backpropagation and attention from first principles. This series builds that intuition better than any textbook.

Fundamentals Transformers From Scratch FREE
~25 hours total Watch Playlist

Let's Build GPT

Andrej Karpathy

10/10

Build a GPT from scratch in a single 2-hour video. Covers tokenization, attention mechanisms, and transformer architecture with live coding. The most-watched LLM tutorial on YouTube.

Why you need this: LLM questions dominate 2024-2025 interviews. Understanding how GPT works under the hood sets you apart from candidates who only use APIs.

LLMs GPT Live Coding FREE
2 hours Watch Video

CS229: Machine Learning

Stanford University (Andrew Ng)

9/10

The legendary Stanford ML course taught by Andrew Ng. Covers supervised learning, unsupervised learning, SVMs, neural networks, and reinfortic learning with rigorous mathematical foundations.

Why you need this: Classic ML concepts (SVMs, PCA, k-means) still appear in interviews. This course provides the mathematical rigor that impresses senior interviewers.

Classic ML Math Heavy Stanford FREE
~30 hours Watch Playlist

CS182: Deep Learning

UC Berkeley

9/10

Berkeley's comprehensive deep learning course covering CNNs, RNNs, transformers, generative models, reinforcement learning, and modern architectures. Excellent balance of theory and practice.

Why you need this: Covers the full spectrum of deep learning with clear explanations. Great preparation for ML system design and architecture discussions.

Deep Learning CNNs/RNNs Berkeley FREE
~40 hours Watch Playlist

Full Stack Deep Learning

FSDL Team (Berkeley Alumni)

9/10

Production ML from Berkeley instructors. Covers MLOps, deployment, monitoring, data management, and building real ML products. Updated annually with latest tools (2024 includes LLMOps).

Why you need this: ML system design interviews focus on production concerns. This course teaches exactly what companies ask: deployment, monitoring, and MLOps best practices.

MLOps Production LLMOps FREE
~20 hours Watch Channel

CS224N: NLP with Deep Learning

Stanford University

9/10

Stanford's premier NLP course covering word vectors, RNNs, attention, transformers, BERT, GPT, and modern LLMs. Taught by Chris Manning, a pioneer in computational linguistics.

Why you need this: NLP is central to modern ML roles. This course provides deep understanding of transformers and language models that interviewers expect.

NLP Transformers LLMs FREE
~35 hours Watch Playlist

CS25: Transformers United

Stanford University

8/10

Cutting-edge seminar featuring talks from researchers at OpenAI, Google, Meta, and Anthropic. Covers latest advances in transformers, multimodal models, and AI agents.

Why you need this: Stay current with latest research. Great for discussing recent advances and showing you follow the cutting edge during interviews.

Cutting Edge Research Seminars FREE
~15 hours Watch Playlist

Building AI Agents (Agentic AI)

Stanford / Industry Experts

9/10

The hottest topic in ML right now! Learn to build autonomous AI agents using LLMs, tool use, planning, and multi-agent systems. Covers LangChain, AutoGPT architectures, and production agent design.

Why you need this: AI agents are the #1 hiring priority in 2024-2025. Companies like OpenAI, Anthropic, and Google are racing to build agentic systems. This knowledge is invaluable.

AI Agents LangChain Hot Topic FREE
~10 hours Search Videos
